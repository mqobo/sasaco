renewable energy and energy recovery capabilities

Our renewable energy and energy recovery capabilities extend across the full spectrum of renewable energy sources which include:

  • Wind and solar
  • Biofuels
  • Energy from waste, including methane gas recovery from landfill sites
  • Ground heat and cold storage

energy cost management

We drive energy efficiency - advising on energy cost management through:

  • Assessment and implementation of energy
  • Energy bench marking
  • Monitoring services as per the energy-saving agreements between utilities and industry

Energy storage

Energy storage is a critical component of certain renewable energy products. We provide sophisticated storage systems in the following fields:

  • Electricity
  • Gas (e.g. import terminals for liquefied natural gas)
  • Oil and subsoil heat/cold

power systems

Our power systems and planning teams, have built enormous experience within the following sectors:

  • Electrical network master plans/network development plans
  • Grid impact and reliability studies
  • Energy planning and modelling (OSeMOSYS, FlexiTool, and PLEXOS)

This has being acheived through working with many Clients from both public and private sector

dedicated distribution sector team

We also have a dedicated distribution sector team, who have experience within the following areas:

  • Revenue management enhancements for electrical utilities
  • Electricification (both on a local and regional point of view)
  • Tarrifs and pricings (for both utilities and municipalities)
