About Sasaco

high quality consulting services

As a 100% Black and youth-owned company, Sasaco is committed to becoming a key player in engineering, energy, and sustainability. We are driven by the immense opportunities in the energy sector and the need for innovative, high-impact solutions. Our focus is on delivering customized, highquality consulting services that align with our clients' unique goals. We are dedicated to strategic growth, technical excellence, and long-term partnerships that drive sustainable progress

With a team of industry-leading professionals, we bring expertise in:

  • Registered Professional Engineers (Electrical, Civil, Mechanical)
  • Certified Energy Managers (CEM)
  • Project Management Professionals (PMP)
  • Certified Environmental & H&S Specialists
  • Certified Financial Modeling Experts (Renewable Energy)

At Sasaco, we don't just consult — we create solutions that shape the future. We look forward to working with you.
