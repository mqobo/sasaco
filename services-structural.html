Sasaco provides specialized structural engineering and asset management solutions tailored to mining, infrastructure, and industrial sectors. We focus on ensuring the resilience, durability, and longevity of critical assets, helping clients extend asset life, enhance safety, and optimize operational performance.

We offer a comprehensive approach to structural assessments, remediation, and future-proofing, ensuring that infrastructure meets current industry standards while preparing for future operational demands.

Our expertise includes:

  • Steel Structure Assessment & Remediation - Conducting detailed inspections to identify vulnerabilities and applying targeted solutions to enhance strength and durability
  • Concrete Repair & Rehabilitation - Addressing structural deterioration through advanced restoration techniques that improve integrity and extend the lifespan of concrete assets.
  • Structural Strengthening & Retrofitting - Upgrading existing infrastructure to support increased loads, modern operational requirements, and regulatory compliance.
  • Deterioration & Condition Assessments - Utilizing advanced diagnostic tools to analyze wear, corrosion, and material degradation, providing data-driven recommendations for proactive asset management.
