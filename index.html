Our Health & Safety Philosophy

We underpin our strong culture of health and safety with a detailed, targeted and responsive health and safety governance framework and management system. Through application of our health and safety management system, our goal is to:

  • Provide a safe and healthy work environment for everyone associated with us;
  • Provide practical tools and techniques for use in the assessment and consideration of health and safety issues across all work we undertake;
  • Provide expertise and resources to implement and maintain our health and safety policies, standards and procedures;
  • Provide health and safety management processes to anyone working on a project to assist in:
    • eliminating hazards;
    • preventing incidents, assessing, managing, controlling and reducing risk comprehensively;
    • managing injury and rehabilitation; and
    • promoting health, safety, wellbeing and continual improvements.

Our dedication to social change

At Sasaco, we recognize the challenges faced by students, especially those from disadvantaged backgrounds, in transitioning from high school to university—a shift that has become even more complex with the rise of online learning.

To bridge this gap, Sasaco, in collaboration with partners in education and human resource development, is committed to supporting university students by providing mentorship, skills development, and structured support systems that enhance their academic journey and future career prospects.

Through this initiative, we aim to empower young talent, foster inclusivity, and contribute to building a skilled workforce that drives innovation and progress in Africa.
